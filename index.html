~how to get any blackboard video as an mp4 (chrome)

  • go to blackboard page which contains the mp4
  • click inspect for chosen video, don't play the video
  • click network, then select media within inspect panel
  • click play on the video, an item should appear within the panel
  • right click item + open in new tab
  • video with no blackboard embed, download or use the link to view from anywhere without login
